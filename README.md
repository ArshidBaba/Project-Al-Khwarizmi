# Project-Al-Khwarizmi

## This project is an attempt to bring togehter ideas, concepts, problems and their potential solutions from diverse but connected areas of research including but not limited to computer science, mathematics, physics, philosophy, information theory and biology. 
## The main problems discussed here would be touching on universal computation, computational theoretic view of the universe and mind, information theoretic view of the universe and mind. 
## The limitations of computation and mathematics and their implications on the nature of reality and the working of conciousness.
